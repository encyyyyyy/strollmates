Strollmates Support

For app support, safety reports, privacy questions, or account deletion help, email nickconoplia@icloud.com.

Common Help

  • Use Settings > Privacy to change live sharing or cloud backup.
  • Use Settings > Strollmates+ to restore purchases or manage subscriptions.
  • Use friend and shared-walk action menus to report or block another user.
  • Use Settings > Account > Delete account to remove your account and reset onboarding.
