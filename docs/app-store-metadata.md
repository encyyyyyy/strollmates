## Description

Strollmates is a walking tracker that turns everyday walks into a shared map you can build with friends.

Track routes, distance, steps, and walking time. Reveal your local fog map as you explore, save your walking history, and follow neighborhood progress over time. You can keep everything private, or choose to share live walks with accepted friends.

Free features include:
- Personal walk tracking
- Route maps and walking history
- Fog map coverage and neighborhood progress
- Step and distance stats
- Privacy controls for sharing
- Optional Apple Health step and distance reading
- Account deletion from Settings

Strollmates+ is an optional auto-renewable subscription purchased with Apple's in-app purchase system. It unlocks social walking features including live friend tracking, friend requests, shared walk activity, friend leaderboards, advanced stats, and extra map controls. Subscription details, duration, renewal, trials, and cancellation options are shown before purchase. You can restore purchases and manage subscriptions in Settings.

Permissions:
- Location is used to draw walk routes, calculate distance, reveal map coverage, and share live walks only according to your sharing settings.
- Motion is used for step and activity estimates.
- Apple Health is optional and used only to read step and distance data when you choose to connect it.

Safety and privacy:
Strollmates includes friend request controls, blocking, reporting, and in-app account deletion. Your shared walks are visible only according to your privacy settings and accepted friend relationships.
